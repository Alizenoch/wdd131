Port Moresby,Papua New Guinea

Port Moresby, is the capital city of Papua New Guinea. It is a nation situated in the Pacific between Australia and Indonesia.It's official language is English and tok pisin is locally spoken as the main languges apart from the 800 plus different language of different tribes. It gained it's independence from Australia in 1975. It has 22 provinces and the second largest city is Lae.The country is rich in natural resources and diverse culture heritage however poorly managed.
